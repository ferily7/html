Members

Yoon Doojoon

Yong Junhyung

  • Birthday: December 19th, 1989

  • Junhyung has composed and produced many of BEAST/Highlight's song. He has also composed music for other artists too

Yang Yoseob

  • Yoseob is one of the best vocalist out of all the Korean singers and participates in soundtracks for Korean dramas and plays in many musicals

Lee Gikwang

Son Dongwoon
